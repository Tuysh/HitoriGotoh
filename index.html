Hitori Gotoh

Hitori Gotoh is a first-year high school student and a guitarist of the band Kessoku Band. She is a socially awkward and introverted girl who struggles with severe social anxiety. Despite her shyness, Hitori is passionate about music and talented at playing the guitar.
